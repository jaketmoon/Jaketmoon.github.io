English Expressions

  1. “Fighting back the tears”

    • In Sentence: “Fighting back the tears, he spoke proudly of the fact…”
    • Explanation: Trying hard to stop oneself from crying.
    • Synonym: Holding back tears
    • Example Sentence: She was fighting back the tears as she spoke about her late grandmother.

  2. Golden years”

    • In Sentence: “For them and some older Americans, their so-called “golden years” are at times not so pleasant…”
    • Explanation: A period of time after retirement, supposed to be enjoyable and relaxing.
    • Synonym: Retirement years
    • Example Sentence: Many look forward to their golden years to travel and pursue hobbies.

  3. “From the ground up”

    • In Sentence: “He spoke proudly of the fact that he had built their home from the ground up…”
    • Explanation: From the beginning; from scratch.
    • Synonym: From scratch
    • Example Sentence: She built her business from the ground up, turning it into a successful enterprise.

  4. “In a forceful manner”

  • In Sentence: “He exclaimed in a forceful manner that he felt he wasn’t important anymore.”
  • Explanation: In a way that shows strong feelings or conviction.
  • Synonym: Assertively
  • Example Sentence: She argued her point in a forceful manner, convincing everyone in the room.

  1. “Undernourishment”

    • In Sentence: “Undernourishment during this approximately 1,000-day window can seriously check the growth and development of children…”
    • Explanation: The condition of not having enough food or not enough of the right kind of food for good health and growth.
    • Synonym: Malnutrition
    • Example Sentence: Undernourishment in early childhood can lead to long-term health issues.

  2. “On board”

    • In Sentence: “This is why we’re all on board in focusing on those thousand days to improve nutrition.”
    • Explanation: In agreement with or part of a group or team.
    • Synonym: In agreement
    • Example Sentence: Everyone is on board with the plan to renovate the old school building.

  3. “High value-to-weight ratio”

  • In Sentence: “Suitable products generally have a high value-to-weight ratio.”
  • Explanation: Refers to items that are valuable but not very heavy.
  • Synonym: Cost efficiency
  • Example Sentence: Jewelry has a high value-to-weight ratio, making it expensive despite its small size.
  1. “Purely virtual environment”
  • In Sentence: “Digital products, such as software, films, and music, can be sold in a purely virtual environment.”
  • Explanation: A setting or place that exists only online, not in the physical world.
  • Synonym: Digital platform
  • Example Sentence: Many companies operate in a purely virtual environment, reducing overhead costs.
    • “Market leader”
    • In Sentence: “Do this and you can easily become the market leader, as Skype has proved.”
    • Explanation: A company or product that has the largest share of sales in a particular market.
    • Synonym: Industry leader
    • Example Sentence: With its innovative approach, the company quickly became the market leader in renewable energy solutions.

  2. “Critical first thousand days”

- **In Sentence:** "Nations will not make progress fighting hunger and poverty until they begin to focus on those critical first thousand days."
- **Explanation:** A significant period from conception to a child's second birthday that is crucial for development and health.
- **Synonym:** Vital early period
- **Example Sentence:** Investing in nutrition during the critical first thousand days can have lifelong benefits for children.

English-Chinese Translation

  1. “It gives me great pleasure to introduce our keynote speaker”
    • Chinese Translation: 很高兴为大家介绍我们今天的主题演讲人
  2. “He had built their home from the ground up…”
    • Chinese Translation: 他从头开始建造了他们的家…
  3. “The prospect of having to sell their home was an extremely painful experience.”
    • Chinese Translation: 不得不出售他们的家的前景是一次极其痛苦的经历。
  4. “Their so-called ‘golden years’ are at times not so pleasant.”
    • Chinese Translation: 他们所谓的“黄金岁月”有时并不那么愉快。
  5. “Undernourishment can seriously check the growth and development of children.”
    • Chinese Translation: 营养不良会严重阻碍孩子们的成长和发展。
  6. “They will be more likely to perform well in school.”
    • Chinese Translation: 他们在学校的表现更有可能优秀。
  7. “Suitable products generally have a high value-to-weight ratio.”
    • Chinese Translation: 合适的产品通常具有高价值重量比。
  8. “Digital products can be sold in a purely virtual environment.”
    • Chinese Translation: 数字产品可以在纯虚拟环境中出售。
  9. “Products which are potentially embarrassing to buy do well in the virtual environment.”
    • Chinese Translation: 在虚拟环境中，可能令人尴尬的产品销售情况良好。
